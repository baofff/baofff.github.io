Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student of TSAIL Group in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jun Zhu.

My research interest includes machine learning and deep learning. Recently, I am interested in deep generative models and hyperparameter optimization.
